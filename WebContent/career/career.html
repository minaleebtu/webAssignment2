Intake Nurse/Nurse Manager

  • Job Description
    Responsible for coordination of agency intake process, effective allocation of staff to service patients, liaison with referral sources and such other clinical duties as may be required.
  • Employment Type
    Full Time - 1st Shift
  • Job Requirements
    • Two years health care experience

Nurse Anesthetist

  • Job Description
    Under the supervision of an anesthesiologist, the Nurse Anesthetist (NA) will perform safe anesthesia care, consisting of the following: pre-operative assessment, writing of appropriate pre-op orders as specified by approved standard procedure, administration of all types of anesthetic and techniques, patient monitoring intra-operatively, provides for intra-op course and emergence and assists as necessary in post-operative care.
  • Employment Type
    Full Time
  • Job Requirements
    • Knowledge of current nursing, anesthesia and critical care techniques

Nurse Practitioner

  • Job Description
    Family nurse practitioner provides healthcare to the full spectrum of the lifespan of patients. Provides patient care, within the scope of practice of the NP license, in the arenas of urgent care and retail care.
  • Employment Type
    Part Time
  • Job Requirements
    • 1-2 Year of demonstrated competency in the skills and knowledge pertinent to the practice of a Family Nurse Practitioner for all patient populations in primary and/or urgent care
    • Masters of Nursing or Doctorate of Nursing Practice, with board certification as Family Nurse Practitioner

Pharmacist

  • Job Description
    Pharmacists are responsible for the safe and accurate dispensing of pharmaceuticals and ensure appropriateness of prescribed therapy for all patient populations including neonates, pediatrics, adolescents, adults and geriatrics.
  • Employment Type
    Per Diem
  • Job Requirements
    • Bachelors of Science in Pharmacy
    • 5 years hospital pharmacy experience if has a Bachelors or Masters in Pharmacy. Prior work experience not required for Doctor of Pharmacy.
    • Pharmacist License

Physician, Dermatology

  • Job Description
    Evaluates and treats patients with appropriate medical diagnostic and treatment skills. Coordinates discharge planning with case management.
  • Employment Type
    Full Time
  • Job Requirements
    • Licensed Physician MD/DO credentialed
    • Doctor of Medicine (MD) or Doctor of Osteopathy (DO)
