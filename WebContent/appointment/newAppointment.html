Make an appointment

Call us 0178 8234660 to make an appointment

 Doctors and nurses see patients by appointment throughout the day. You can make an appointment by phoning, or calling in at reception. If your problem is urgent, we will see you as soon as possible. We aim to give you an appointment with a doctor or nurse of your choice within a week. If you feel that you need more time than usual with a doctor or a nurse, please ask for a DOUBLE APPOINTMENT when you make an appointment.

Booking the right appointment length and arriving on time will help surgeries run smoothly.
