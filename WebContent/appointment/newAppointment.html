Doctors and nurses see patients by appointment throughout the day. You can make an appointment by sending a mail, or calling in at reception.

If your problem is urgent, we will see you as soon as possible. We aim to give you an appointment with a doctor or nurse of your choice within a week.

If you feel that you need more time than usual with a doctor or a nurse, please ask for a DOUBLE APPOINTMENT when you make an appointment.

Please note that we operate the following appointment lengths:

  • Doctors - 10 minutes
  • Nurses - 15 minutes
Booking the right appointment length and arriving on time will help surgeries run smoothly.

Telephone Advice

Many issues do not need a face to face consultation and can be dealt with over the telephone and you can request a telephone consultation. At times of heavy demand when there is no appointment available to meet your request you will be offered a telephone appointment. If the doctor feels that you need to be seen they will arrange a time for you to come in to the practice. This service is offered each morning. The doctor will call you back in the order that requests are received.
