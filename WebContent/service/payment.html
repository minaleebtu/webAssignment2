As a member of a statutory health insurance you are relieved of the concern for the cost. We arrange the expenses for your stay directly with your health insurance; she covers the costs of general hospital services. For a maximum of 28 days of a calendar year - a statutory additional payment is made - by law. The amount of the current daily rate is to be requested at the time of admission; he is currently 10 EURO.

Exempted from this additional payment

  • Patients until the age of 18 years
  • Patients for inpatient delivery
  • Semi-stationary treatments
  • Only pre-stationary treatments
  • Patients for whom a social welfare institution pays the treatment costs
  • Patients for whom statutory accident insurance covers the costs of treatment

Please settle this additional payment for a longer stay after 28 days, otherwise on the discharge day. Cash payments at the health fund of the university hospital are acknowledged.

Please keep this confirmation of payment well if you expect another hospitalization in another clinic in the same year. If you have already paid all or part of your additional payment during another hospital stay, please provide us with the receipt. In this way you facilitate the settlement with the health insurance. self-pay

If you pay for the costs of your stay or take advantage of additional benefits beyond general hospital benefits, you are self-payer. We will then settle the treatment costs directly with you.

You make an advance payment in the amount of the expected costs or costs. For a longer stay we issue interim bills. Prepayments made are offset against the final invoice after the end of your inpatient stay; For in-patient treatment, the day of discharge is not calculated.

In many cases, a private health insurance or - in the case of insured persons - supplementary insurance will bear your costs. However, you are initially obliged to pay, provided that you do not submit a written declaration of acceptance or the hospital card of your private insurance company upon admission.

Please pay all (part) invoices within three weeks; You will find the account numbers and payment modalities under the keyword "cash register".
